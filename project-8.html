Boca Grande

Category : Digital Marketing
Client : Restaurant Boca Grande
Role : Digital Marketing

A place with excellent food and unique dishes that can only be found in Boca Grande. A careful musical selection along with furnishings inspired by barn wood.

More than 3,500 photos of our plates on Instagram, almost 1 in 5 diners has posted something BocaGrande in their social networks.
Time Out Mexico, Animal Gourmet, MX City, Dónde Ir and Trip Advisor have placed BocaGrande among the top 3 places to eat BBQ in CDMX.

Some of my main tasks were:
°Manage the social media community and supervise moderation, daily publications of content, and interactions with clients.
°Prepare action plans and participate in the elaboration of digital and social media strategies.
