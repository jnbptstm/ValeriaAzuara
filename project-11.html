Playa Corona

Category : Events
Client : Beer Corona / Grupo Modelo
Role : Event Manager

Corona it's one of the top-selling beers worldwide, and has been the top-selling imported drink in the U.S. since 1998.

The event took place at Puerto Vallarta with more than 5,000 attendees in 3 days. During the event Corona Extra Beer doubled their sales in the center of the country (Nayarit, Jalisco y Guerrero).

One of my main tasks were:
°In charge of the production and organization of the event (pre-production, during the event and after the event).
°Reports after the event to see the results of the sales.
