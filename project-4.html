Hotel de Leyendas Victoria

Category : Events
Client : Beer Victoria - Grupo Modelo
Role : Event Manager

An immersive experience that represented the Mexican legends of the Day of the Dead. Hotel de Leyendas Victoria took you to relive the legends in a different way.

Successfully event with more than 10,000 attendees to the immersive experience in one month. +3000 m2 intervened in the construction (3 weeks of pre production, 1 month of operation, 1 month after production).
Victoria Beer increased sales by more than 15% in the center of the country. More than 300 Media outlets have attended the event such as Time Out México, Dondé Ir, Enterprenuer, Merca2.0, LovetheWork, etc.
