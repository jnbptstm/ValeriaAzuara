Single Project - Video

Category : Digital Marketing
Client : Restaurant Hotel Casa Awolly
Completion : February 2017
Role : Digital Marketing

An immersive experience that represented the Mexican legends of the Day of the Dead. Hotel de Leyendas Victoria took you to relive the legends in a different way. Hotel Casa Awolly is one of the best restaurants in México City, with more than 16,000 followers on Instagram, and more than 1 million posted photos with the hashtag #hotelcasaawolly. Some of my main tasks with the restaurant were: °Prepare action plans and participate in the elaboration of digital and social media strategies. °Organize and attend events for live streaming. °Measure the weekly activity and produce weekly quality/quantity reports, while adding recommendations. °Direction of photography
