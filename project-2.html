Lucas Local

Category : Social Media / Events
Client : Restaurant Lucas Local
Role : Digital Content & Event Manager

Lucas Local became a place of culinary excellence that gathers art lovers and the creative community. It's a space for film and photography as well as venue for musicians and music lovers to share their favorite tracks.

The #lucaslocal hashtag has generated more than 2,500 photos on Instagram, and more than 5,000 people follows the Lucas Local account, thanks to the atmosphere and presentation of dishes that make a visual feast.

Some of my main activities were:
° Instagram and Facebook content creator, as well as social media visual assets
° Organization of the events.
° Photography design
° Weekly reports to see the growth in social media.
° Monitor and animate conversations on all social media channels.
